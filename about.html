About Villamor Air Base

Our Mission

Villamor Air Base serves as the primary gateway for the Philippine Air Force. Our hub, BanastaoHub, was created to provide a clear directory of the various services, workshops, and historical landmarks found within the base.

Whether you are looking for information on aviation history or seeking to join our local community workshops, we are here to guide you.
